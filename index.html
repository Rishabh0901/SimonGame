Simon Game

Game instructions:

Step1: After pressing any key observe the color block which has flashed in white.
Step2: Click the flashed color block .
Step3: On clicking the block, any color block will flash again. Click the color blocks in the sequence of their flash.
Step4: On clicking any block out of order, the game will Get Over and background will change to red for 1 sec. and the score will be displayed on top.

Press any key to start the game.
